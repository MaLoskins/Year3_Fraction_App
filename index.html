How to Play

Drag and drop the fraction bars into the rows. Try to make the total in each row equal to 1 (a whole). Use the Erase button to remove fractions individually or the Reset button to start over.

Understanding Fractions

The numerator (top number) indicates how many parts you have, while the denominator (bottom number) indicates how many equal parts the whole is divided into.
